About Our College

Established with a vision to revolutionize dental education, Paaniniya Dental College has been a pioneer in oral healthcare education for over two decades.

Founded in 1998, Paaniniya Dental College has grown to become one of the premier dental institutions in the region. Our sprawling 25-acre campus houses state-of-the-art facilities that provide an ideal environment for learning and research.

We pride ourselves on our distinguished faculty comprising experienced clinicians, researchers, and academicians who are committed to mentoring students to achieve professional excellence.

Our curriculum is designed to provide a perfect balance of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ensuring our graduates are practice-ready professionals.
